Media Lab Research

Category: Research

Tools Used: Data Analysis, Processing.js, Python, JavaScript, React.js

Project date: Jan 2023 – May 2024

Project Description

From my freshman spring through sophomore spring, I worked as an undergraduate researcher in both the Affective Computing and Personal Robotics Groups at the MIT Media Lab.

I contributed to four main projects: integrating ChatGPT into an empathetic Wordle-style agent, designing foster youth storytelling apps with and without AI, refining and deploying a Jibo-based storytelling robot, and prototyping wearable biofeedback devices for emotion regulation in individuals with ASD.

I worked on rapid prototyping, user study facilitation, data collection, and creative technical visualization using Processing.js and EmotiBit hardware. I co-ran remote Jibo deployments, conducted workshops, and contributed to a paper accepted to HRI 2025.
