Mechanical Engineering and Design Student

Hi! I'm River, a junior studying Mechanical Engineering with a concentration in Robotic Design at MIT.

I have been spending my time at MIT exploring all sorts of different areas of interest. I have taken classses across the computer science, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and design departments. I have also done research in the MIT Media Lab's Affective Computing and Personal Robotics Group, and I am currently working on a joint research project between the Architecture Department's Self-Assembly Lab and Hyundai Motor Company. I love creating whether through building a website, designing a closed-loop controller, or a 70 feet tall inflatable art installation.

In my freetime, I enjoy organizing events for my living community, East Campus. I have organized a carnival, pumpkin drop, and concert for the dormitory. This past year, I also served as the dorm's president and have recently been elected the next vice president. Outside of government, I like playing guitar, making jewlery, printing stickers, and working as a campus ambassador for Monster Energy!
