The creative process is messy, dysfunctional, and never-ending. When you hit a creative block, there is no telling when it will end. The big idea that made sense one day may seem like trash the next. However, those "flaws" within the creative process are also what makes the process beautiful. Inducing creativity is hard, but through it, you develop a deeper understanding of yourself. The result may not always be stellar, but the results are not what matters: it is all about the process. Maybe none of the projects above are significant or challenging enough to constitute a "final." However, who said a project's worth is all about difficulty? Computer Science is an art form, and in art (and life), there is beauty in the small, simple things. Thank you for visiting my creativity journal, where I log all the small projects that I have made to channel my inner creativity.
